Memories and Experiences

Introduction

Hello, I'am Gelzea "Gelo" Domingo, a fourth year Design Major at UC Davis. A creative graphic artist with a playeful mindset, striving to give my all to learn, be inspired, and improve everyday.

My creativity specializes in creating digital and two-dimensional arts. Producing aesthetically appealing illutrations of making people's lives more entertaining and imaginative.

Memories

Born and raised in the Philippines, I grew up creative talent is what people around me claimed. Upon moving to the United States during my high school days, I got exposure to the significance of expressing identity and influencing others through creativity, inspiring me to pursue a creative profession. In spite of the cultural differences, I managed to find opportunities that shaped my artistic abilities, where I discovered my mischievous insights to produce my pleasing and adorable digital illustrations and designs.

Experiences

People would often underestimate going on the path of a creative profession. I strongly believe having a creative nature, can enhancce ones life journey more enjoyable for the reason that it enables an individual to express themselves and discover imaginative views about life, and I try to convey those messages through my artwork and visual designs.

Initially, I considered creating arts and design as a mere hobby until people became more intrigued and amazed by my creativity, which inspired me to proactively create. As a result, I got recognized and often relied upon for any art-related events by my family, peers, and teachers. In realizing the impact of my skills on people around me, I became motivated to develop and improve my creative capabilities, leading me to study visual performing arts. And I also want people to realize that thoughts as well.

The Extras In My Life

Extras

Bearing a creative nature requires me to embrace new adventures with open arms. Thus, I must continually discover and attempt chances of growing because attaining mastery requires continuous learning. I try to experiment by attempting all sorts of creative work methods, such as realistic portraits, landscapes, surreal patterns, photography, editing, etc. I try to make my art and design reflect how I see the world and my lifestyle, as well as how I perceive it from the inside or my essence, endeavoring to represent symbolic and conceptual glimpses around me.
